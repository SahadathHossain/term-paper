## How to Use These References

### For In-Text Citations
When citing in the paper, use the author-date format:
- Single author: (Author, Year)
- Multiple authors: (Author1 & Author2, Year) or (Author1, Author2, & Author3, Year)
- More than three authors: (Author et al., Year)

### For Quotes
Include page numbers: (Author, Year, p. XX) or (Author, Year, pp. XX-XX)
